Privacy Policy for First Marker

Effective Date: 04/06/2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Uploaded Documents: Images or scans of test scripts and any text extracted via OCR.
  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics like feature usage, app crashes, device type, and OS.

2. User Responsibilities and Student Privacy

  • Do not upload student PII (e.g., names, photos).
  • Use pseudonyms or ID numbers for students.
  • Obtain necessary institutional permissions.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Analyze scanned content with AI for grading.
  • Store results locally or with optional cloud sync.
  • We do not sell, advertise, or train AI on your data without consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is encrypted and stored locally or in the cloud (if enabled). No system is 100% secure, so sensitive info should be avoided.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted partners (e.g., Google Vision, OpenRouter, GPT-4) that comply with data protection laws.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is for educators. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and delete your data anytime.
  • Email us to request data removal if needed.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Continued use indicates agreement with the changes.

9. Contact Us

Email us at skooltchdev@gmail.com with any questions.
